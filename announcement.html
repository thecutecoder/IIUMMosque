APPLICATION TO PERFORM FRIDAY CONGREGATIONAL PRAYER ON 31st JULY 2020 AT IIUM SHAS MOSQUE GOMBAK CAMPUS

Please be informed that the Sultan Haji Ahmad Shah Mosque, CENTRIS, Gombak Campus would like to invite all members of IIUM Community to submit the application to perform friday congregational prayer on 31st July 2020 through Google form. The dateline to submit application is until Wednesday at 03:00 pm. Please be reminded that only successful applicants will be notified through email. Please be mindful that the time for ENTERING to the Mosque is between 12.20 pm - 13.00 pm. only.

All worshippers (Jamaah) must register their name at the counter, use hand sanitizer and undergo body temperature screening at the entrance area every time before entering the mosque.

May Allah Subhāna-hu wa Tacālā bless all of us and place reward of our good efforts in the Mizan al-Hasanat.

Thank you.

CONTRIBUTION FOR IIUM SHAS MOSQUE (ISM), CENTRIS, GOMBAK CAMPUS AND RAMADHAN PROGRAMMES 1441H/ 2020M.

We have the pleasure to announce that the ISM, CENTRIS will be organising programmes to foster the sublime spirit of the holy month, Ramadhan 1441H/ 2020M. Among the programmes are Tarawih, Qiyām Al-Layl, Tadarus al-Qur’an, Qur’an Memorization Competition, Tadzkirah and Lectures and Daily Iftār, Sahur and Moreh in jamaah.

As for Iftar programme (inclusive Moreh and Sahur), the ISM, CENTRIS is planning to prepare meals for about 500 to 1,000 students (out of expected number of 5,000 during semester II, 2019/2020 session on campus). This would incur the cost of about RM 150,000 per month. We are looking forward to your sincere contribution in this regard. Your contribution not only feeds the students but also strengthens their relationship with Allah Subhānahu wa Tacālā. And the ties of Islamic brotherhood (al-Ukhuwwah al-Islamiyyah) among themselves, and thus promoting a harmonious God fearing community at IIUM.

For inquiries, please liaise with Br. Badrul Shahri Basri or Sr. Rohana Saad at extension 5907/ 5904 or email badruls@iium.edu.my/harna@iium.edu.my

May Allah Subhāna-hu wa Tacālā bless all of us and place reward of our good efforts in the Mizan al-Hasanat.

Thank you.
