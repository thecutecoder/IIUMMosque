Frequently asked questions

Topic: Fiqh

  1. What is Fiqh? -the theory or philosophy of Islamic law, based on the teachings of the Koran and the traditions of the Prophet.
  2. What is the importance of Fiqh?- Fiqh expands and develops Shariah through interpretation (ijtihad) of the Quran and Sunnah
  3. What is shariah? -Islamic law, based on the Quran and on the sunnah of the Prophet
